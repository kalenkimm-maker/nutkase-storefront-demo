S2000 & Skyus 160 — Key Features
🛰️

Integrated GPS / GNSS

Both units include integrated GPS/GNSS receivers — track asset location, log movement history, and trigger geofence alerts without external GPS hardware.

📟

RS-232 Serial (Skyus 160)

The Skyus 160 bridges legacy RS-232 serial equipment to cellular networks — connect flow meters, RTUs, and monitoring equipment that hasn't changed in decades.

DIN-Rail & Panel Mount

Standard DIN-rail mounting integrates both units directly into industrial control panels alongside PLCs, breakers, and other rail-mounted equipment.

Accept a range of DC voltages common in industrial environments — vehicle power, solar systems, and UPS-backed panel power all work without converters.
