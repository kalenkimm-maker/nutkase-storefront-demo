Amazon Custom

How Custom Ordering Works

Amazon Custom lets you personalize NutKase products directly on Amazon — no separate quoting process, no waiting for a sales call.

Step 1
Select a Case & Upload
Find any NutKase listing with the "Customize now" button. Upload your logo file (PNG, JPG, or SVG), choose placement, and select a case color.
Step 2
Preview Your Design
Amazon's live customization preview shows exactly how your logo looks on the case before you place the order. Adjust size and position until it looks right.
Step 3
Order & Ship
Add to cart and check out on Amazon as usual. NutKase prints and ships your custom cases directly. Bulk orders receive volume pricing automatically.

Customization Options

What You Can Personalize

Amazon Custom supports three types of product personalization. NutKase offers all three across its case lineup.

Logo Upload
Upload your organization's logo in any standard format. NutKase prints it in full color on the case surface. Placement is configurable — front, back, or spine.
Case Color Selection
Choose from NutKase's standard color palette to match your brand identity. Select the case body color independently from the printed logo color.
Text & Labeling
Add a department name, asset number, or custom text to each case. Useful for device management in schools, hospitals, and large fleet deployments.

Questions

Common Questions

What file formats are accepted for logo upload?
Amazon Custom accepts PNG, JPG, and SVG files. For best print quality, NutKase recommends a vector SVG or high-resolution PNG at 300 DPI or higher. Logos with transparent backgrounds produce the cleanest results.
Is there a minimum order quantity?
No. Amazon Custom removes the traditional MOQ requirement — you can order a single custom case or hundreds. Bulk pricing tiers apply automatically at higher quantities.
How long does fulfillment take?
Custom orders are fulfilled directly by NutKase. Production typically takes 3–5 business days, with shipping time on top. Estimated delivery is shown at checkout before you confirm the order.
Can I order different logos on different cases in the same order?
Each unit in an order can be configured individually. You can mix cases with different logos, colors, or text within a single Amazon order — useful for multi-department deployments.
Are returns accepted on custom orders?
Personalized products are generally non-returnable unless there is a defect or production error. NutKase will replace any case where the print quality does not match the approved preview.
