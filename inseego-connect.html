Platform Overview

Everything Your IT Team Needs. Nothing They Don't.

Inseego Connect replaces fragmented per-device management tools with a unified API-first platform that scales from 10 devices to 1.2 million — with the same interface and the same workflows.

Core Capabilities
🚀

Zero-Touch Provisioning

Pre-configure devices before they ship — field teams open the box and the device connects, authenticates, and downloads its configuration automatically. No IT on-site required.

📊

Real-Time Device Analytics

Signal strength, data consumption, connected device count, uptime, and battery level — visible in real time for every device in the fleet from a single dashboard.

🔧

Remote Diagnostics

Diagnose and resolve connectivity issues remotely — reboot, reconfigure, run diagnostics, or change carrier profiles without dispatching a technician.

📦

Over-the-Air Firmware Management

Push firmware updates to individual devices or the entire fleet on a schedule — keep every device current and eliminate the security risk of outdated firmware.

🔐

Role-Based Access Control

Define granular permissions for IT admins, field managers, and read-only viewers — with enterprise SSO integration via SAML 2.0 and LDAP/Active Directory.

🌐

API-First Architecture

Open REST APIs enable integration with ServiceNow, Jira, Splunk, and any ITSM or SIEM system — automate workflows and surface Inseego data where your team already works.

📋

Policy & Profile Management

Create connectivity profiles, SSID settings, data cap policies, and firewall rules centrally — push them to individual devices, groups, or the entire fleet instantly.

🔔

Alerting & Notifications

Configurable alerts for device offline events, data threshold violations, signal degradation, and security anomalies — delivered via email, SMS, or webhook.

FIPS 140-3 Device Integration

Manage FIPS 140-3 certified hotspots and gateways through Connect — cryptographic policy enforcement flows from the platform down to the hardware security module.

TAA Compliant Hardware

Zero-Trust Network Access

Enforce device identity, user identity, and context-based access policies across the entire managed fleet — consistent with NIST 800-207 zero-trust architecture principles.

Flexible Deployment Models

Inseego Connect supports multiple deployment architectures to meet your sovereignty, latency, and compliance requirements.

☁️

SaaS (Multi-Tenant Cloud)

The fastest path to deployment — hosted, managed, and maintained by Inseego. Ideal for commercial enterprises and organizations without dedicated cloud infrastructure.

🏢

Private Cloud (GovCloud)

Deploy Connect on your organization's Azure Government or AWS GovCloud tenant — full data sovereignty with Inseego software running in your controlled environment.

🖥️

On-Premises (Air-Gapped)

For classified or air-gapped networks — Inseego Connect can be deployed entirely on-premises, managing devices without any external cloud dependency.
